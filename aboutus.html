Purpose

The purpose of this project is to provide students and individuals with an immersive and productive background music experience. We believe that music is the universal language of harmony, connecting hearts and souls. Our website aims to offer a seamless platform where users can explore a variety of music genres, from soothing lo-fi beats to energizing synthwave melodies. Whether you're studying, working, relaxing, or simply looking to enhance your daily activities, our curated collection of live radio streams and music tracks is here to accompany you. Feel free to choose the music that resonates with your mood and needs. Welcome to a world where melodies create a backdrop for your journey. Furthermore, this project has served as an opportunity for me to enhance my front-end development skills. As I crafted this website, my goal was not only to create a useful tool for music enthusiasts but also to continuously improve my web development capabilities. With each line of code, I've aimed to refine my design, interactivity, and coding techniques, making this project a journey of both music and learning. Additionally, I've included a link to a Pomodoro focus website for users who prefer the Pomodoro technique for studying, further enhancing their productivity and focus.
